A bit about us

IT support the way we'd want it ourselves

We started OS Technology because we wanted to build an IT company we'd actually want to use. Small enough that you're not lost in the system, experienced enough to handle whatever you throw at us. We're based in East Riding of Yorkshire and support clients locally and across the UK.

We're upfront about what things cost and realistic about what we can do. If something can't be fixed, we'll tell you. If you don't actually need a service we offer, we'll tell you that too. We're not here to upsell, we're here to help.

Most of our clients stick around, which we think says more than anything on this page. We work with home users and small businesses across the UK, and we try to make sure you're dealing with the same people each time, not a different face every call.
