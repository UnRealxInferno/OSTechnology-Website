About Us

Microsoft 365 Partner & MSP for Home Users and Growing Businesses

OS Technology was founded on a simple belief: great IT support should be accessible to everyone, not just large enterprises. We're a certified Microsoft 365 partner and managed IT provider, specialising in home users, home businesses, and small & medium companies that are ready to grow.

Whether you're a sole trader setting up professional email for the first time, or an established business that needs the full power of Microsoft 365, we'll set up and run the right plan for you, no pressure to fit a one-size-fits-all box.

Our engineers bring years of hands-on experience across Microsoft 365, cloud infrastructure, and cybersecurity. As a fully remote team, we support clients UK-wide. No call centres, no travel delays, just real engineers who care.
