About Us

Google Workspace Partner & MSP for Home Users and Growing Businesses

Unreal Technology was founded on a simple belief: great IT support should be accessible to everyone, not just large enterprises. We're a certified Google Workspace partner and managed IT provider, specialising in home users, home businesses, and small & medium companies that are ready to grow.

Whether you're a sole trader setting up professional email, a home business getting your first IT system in place, or an expanding SMB that needs a reliable technology partner, we're built for you.

Our engineers bring years of hands-on experience across Google Workspace, cloud infrastructure, and cybersecurity. As a fully remote team, we support clients UK-wide. No call centres, no travel delays, just real engineers who care.
