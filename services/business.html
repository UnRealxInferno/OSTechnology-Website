Google or Microsoft?

What's Right for My Business?

We deploy and manage both Google Workspace and Microsoft 365. Here's how to tell which platform fits where your business is today, and we'll happily talk it through with you.

Google Workspace

Best for new & growing businesses

  • Lower cost — the more affordable entry point for sole traders, start-ups and home businesses.
  • Simple & quick — works in any browser, on any device, with very little to learn or maintain.
  • Built for collaboration — real-time Docs, Sheets and Slides plus generous Drive storage.
  • Low overhead — no servers, no complex licensing, ideal when you don't have in-house IT.

Microsoft 365

Best for professional & medium-to-large businesses

  • Full desktop Office — the complete Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook apps your business relies on.
  • Advanced security & compliance — Entra ID, Conditional Access, Defender and Intune for regulated and growing teams.
  • Deep integration — works hand-in-hand with Windows and existing line-of-business software.
  • Scales with you — centralised identity and device management for larger, established teams.
