Why Microsoft Copilot?

Microsoft Copilot is the AI assistant built directly into the Microsoft 365 apps your team already knows, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook and Teams. It drafts and summarises documents and emails, helps analyse spreadsheet data, and pulls together meeting notes and action points, without asking your team to learn a new tool.

Because it works within your existing Microsoft 365 tenant, we can set it up using the same accounts, permissions and security controls (Entra ID, Conditional Access) that already protect your business, rather than bolting on something separate and harder to manage.

Using AI Safely and Legally

This is the part most businesses skip, and it's the part we care about most. Public, consumer AI tools are convenient, but pasting client details, contracts or personal data into them carries real risk: you often don't know where that data goes or how it's used.

Microsoft 365 Copilot is different. It operates inside your existing Microsoft 365 tenant boundary, and under Microsoft's current commercial data protection terms, it does not use your organisation's prompts or data to train its underlying AI models. That's a documented part of how the product is built, not a promise we're making on Microsoft's behalf.

We help you configure Copilot properly, and just as importantly, we help your team understand what's safe to put into AI tools and what isn't. For most businesses that means a short, plain-English AI usage policy covering approved tools and sensitive data, put together as part of your rollout, supporting your UK GDPR obligations rather than guaranteeing compliance on its own. If your compliance needs are more complex, we'd always recommend involving your own legal or data protection adviser alongside our technical setup.
