Privacy-First Collaboration

NEXTCLOUD Private Cloud for Business

A private alternative to Microsoft 365 for organisations that want complete control and ownership of their business data.

Why Choose NEXTCLOUD?

NEXTCLOUD gives you a privacy-focused collaboration platform with full control over where data lives, how it is accessed, and how long it is retained. It is ideal for businesses with stricter governance, compliance, or internal security requirements.

Compared with per-user SaaS licensing, NEXTCLOUD can often reduce cost per user. However, large storage requirements can increase total cost when cloud infrastructure, backups, and performance scaling are factored in.

What We Deliver

  • Private NEXTCLOUD deployment and hardening
  • User, group and permission architecture
  • File sharing policies and external sharing controls
  • Backup, disaster recovery and update management
  • Migration planning from Google Workspace or Microsoft 365

Right-Fit Assessment

We provide side-by-side guidance so you can choose between Microsoft 365 and NEXTCLOUD based on privacy goals, collaboration needs, storage usage, and long-term operational cost.
